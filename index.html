Notification Logic
Telegram should send alerts only for actionable conditions so the chat will not be flooded by normal readings.

Agroforestry: send Codes 1, 2, 3, and 4.
AWD: alert near -15 cm and upon irrigation trigger.
Rice-Fish: alert for out-of-range water depth or low dissolved oxygen.
